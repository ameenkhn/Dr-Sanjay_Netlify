Practice Overview

Professional and friendly ENT care in Pallavaram.

CHESS is a place for high-quality, professional, and friendly ENT care, presented with clarity and a patient-first structure.

CHESS Surgical Center is positioned as a small, specialized ENT day care center where ear, nose, throat, skull base, balance, hearing, and voice concerns are handled with focused attention rather than broad generalization.

The core approach is simple: patients should feel heard, understand their treatment options, and receive a plan tailored to the specific problem in front of them.

  • Professional ENT specialists with focused clinical and surgical expertise.
  • Quality-assured care with a strong emphasis on precision and patient comfort.
  • Minimally invasive and recovery-aware pathways wherever possible.
